Inscription

This information is very important. Please read it very carefully. Thank you!

- To start:
1. Find the desired sketch, picture, drawing. If you cannot find the sketch which you need, contact me and explain clearly what you want. I'll create a sketch for you.
2. Specify the place where you want to have a tattoo, and its dimensions in centimeters.
3. Wait for my answer.

- How I work:

1. I do Not work with Chinese stuff and with Chinese trash. All my ink is American. Equipment of European manufacture.

2. I always work with music and only with my own. Therefore, just in case, take your headphones with you.

3. I don’t do tattoos to anyone under the age of 18!

4. I usually work always in the morning, assigning time for 11-12-13 hours. Very rarely you can get for the session in the evening.

5. I do not work in debt. The scheme in style of "Do it, and I'll pay later" doesn't work and never will.

> Cover-up:
Basically people want to cover something massive and they do not realize that they have the only one way - it's a removal laser or lightning sessions. I don’t do it. (For the moment) If you need to cover-up a small tattoo, please send me a photo (in good quality) and I'll tell you whether I'll do it or not and whether it's possible to cover it properly without a laser.

> Price:
The cost of work will depend on the size and difficulty of your design. After you send me your idea, I'll write you an approximate time for its implementation and it’s price.

> Inscription:
Once we are determined with the work you need, we’ll pick up the suitable time for you. If you can’t make it or in case of any emergencies, please let me know the day before and not 10 minutes before the scheduled time for the session.
