1. Is it painful?

- No, but it's not always a pleasure as well.

2. How safe is it to make a tattoo?

- If you follow all your tattoo master’s advices, it’s 99.9% safe.

3. I'm very afraid of shots, can I endure a tattoo?

- Yes. Despite the fact that the tattooing process is an incredibly fast injection process, it feels more like a cut than a prick.

4. I want a tattoo, but I don’t know which one.

- So you are not ready for a tattoo yet.

5. I have an old tattoo, I want to cover it or do a refresh, is it possible?

- All depends on the quality, size and how much time passed. You can refresh everything, but not cover. Send a photo of your tattoo (in good quality), I'll tell you what we can do there.

6. Tattoo is expensive, I can not afford it.

- Sure. tattooing is an expensive pleasure. However once paid, you have a picture on your body for the rest of your life. And even for 2 weeks after the death :) Remember this.

7. Why do you use vaseline in your work?

- Vaseline does not allow the tattoo ink to spread over the skin, which greatly simplifies the work. The needle slides smoothly, and in case of bleeding, vaseline helps to prevent blood from drying out.

8. What means «Minimal price» for a tattoo?

- The minimal price is money which you pay for consumable stuff, for disinfection and sterilization, for electricity. The master, with this amount of money, almost does not earn anything.

9. Why can not you drink alcohol before a tattoo session?

- Alcohol disperses blood, so during the session you will bleed more than necessary. It interferes with the work, and it’s not pleasant for you as well.

10. Can I do a temporary tattoo?

- No. Because temporary tattoos - Does Not Exist. Everything that is temporarily held on the body is called a drawing. Technically, tattoo isn’t drawing.
